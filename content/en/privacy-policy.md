### Tracking Technologies and Cookies
We use Cookies and similar tracking tech to keep an eye on activity within our service and to store some info. These tracking tools include beacons, tags, and scripts – all designed to gather and track data, and to help us level up and analyze our service. Here's a peek at the tech we might use:

- **Cookies or Browser Cookies**: Think of a Cookie as a tiny file placed on your device. You can tell your browser to ditch all Cookies or to give you a heads-up when one's being sent. But, heads up! If you don't accept Cookies, some parts of our service might not work for you. Unless you've tweaked your browser settings to reject 'em, our service will likely use Cookies.

- **Web Beacons**: Some parts of our service and our emails might have tiny electronic files called web beacons (you might also hear them called clear gifs, pixel tags, or single-pixel gifs). These little guys let us, for example, count users who've visited those pages or opened an email, and they're useful for other website stats too (like tracking how popular a section is or checking system and server integrity).

Cookies come in two main flavors: "Persistent" and "Session" Cookies. Persistent Cookies chill on your personal computer or mobile device even after you go offline, but Session Cookies vanish the moment you close your web browser. Poof! 💨

We roll with both Session and Persistent Cookies for these reasons:

- **Necessary / Essential Cookies**
  - **Type**: Session Cookies
  - **Administered by**: Us
  - **Purpose**: These Cookies are absolutely crucial for delivering the services you find on our Website and letting you use certain features. They're key for authenticating users and stopping any shady, fraudulent use of accounts. Without these, we simply can't provide the services you've requested, so we use them just for that!

- **Cookies Policy / Notice Acceptance Cookies**
  - **Type**: Persistent Cookies
  - **Administered by**: Us
  - **Purpose**: These Cookies simply remember if you've already given us the nod to use cookies on the Website.

- **Functionality Cookies**
  - **Type**: Persistent Cookies
  - **Administered by**: Us
  - **Purpose**: These Cookies are super handy because they help us remember your choices when you're using the Website – things like your login deets or your preferred language. The whole point is to give you a more personalized experience, so you don't have to keep re-entering your preferences every single time you visit. Sweet!

Want more deets on the Cookies we use and what choices you have? Head over to our Cookies Policy or check out the Cookies section in this very Privacy Policy.
